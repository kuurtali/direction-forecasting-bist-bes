Limitations & Honest Reporting
21 / 24
Sample size
~260 weekly observations across 5 years â€” bootstrap CIs reported but small-sample variance dominates.
Pooled vs Mean
Pooled accuracy (84.38 %) > 3-seed mean (80.21 %). Mean is the headline; pooled is fully disclosed.
Single market
TEFAS only; cross-market transfer to NASDAQ/EuroStoxx left for future work.
Cost-aware loss
Class_weight balancing applied, but not full asymmetric utility (e.g., max-drawdown-aware loss).
